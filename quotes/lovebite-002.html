Overview

What We're Building

A mobile-first web application to manage in-person speed dating events, replacing the current manual process with a simple, private, and scalable digital experience.

Scope

Functional Scope

For Participants

  • QR check-in: upon arriving at the event, they scan a QR code that takes them directly to the event app.
  • Quick registration: name, phone number, gender, real-time selfie (no gallery upload), and creation of a 4-digit PIN to access later.
  • Profile browsing: during the event, each participant sees profiles of the opposite gender with photo and name.
  • Preference selection: for each person they choose between Match (romantic), Friend (friendship), or Not Interested.
  • Results the next day: they log in with phone number + PIN and see only mutual matches.

Privacy Model — Key Feature

  • In a mutual match, the woman automatically receives the man's phone number.
  • The man only knows there was a match if the woman decides to contact him.
  • If the woman does nothing, the match remains invisible to the man, reducing pressure and giving the woman full control.

For the Organizer (Admin Panel)

  • View of all event participants.
  • Match processing with one click: the system evaluates all mutual selections and publishes the results.
  • Access to phones and profiles of all attendees.
