Overview

Project Summary

New Business

Illawarra Star Auto Parts is a new auto parts business being launched as an independent brand, separate from George's Auto Prestige.

The website will be a lightweight, mobile-friendly enquiry site for retail and trade customers looking for car parts across the Illawarra, South Coast, Sydney, and Australia-wide shipping.

The goal is not to build an ecommerce store or inventory system at this stage. The site will focus on clear positioning, trust, service area coverage, and a detailed parts enquiry form that sends directly to info@illawarrastarautoparts.com.

Recommended scope: a standalone website with strong local and trade positioning, designed to collect detailed parts enquiries so the team can respond by email with availability, pricing, pickup, delivery, or shipping options.

Direction

Brand & Market Positioning

  • Independent brand: Illawarra Star Auto Parts will be presented as its own business, without direct linkage to George's Auto Prestige.
  • Customer types: the site will cater to both retail customers and trade/business customers.
  • Service area: Illawarra, South Coast, Sydney, and Australia-wide shipping.
  • Fulfilment options: pickup, local delivery, and shipping across Australia.
  • Commercial focus: enquiries first, fast communication second, online checkout later only if the business decides to expand into ecommerce.
Design direction depends on final branding decisions: logo, colour palette, photo style, and whether the site should feel dark/premium like George's Auto Prestige or more modern, industrial, and trade-focused.

Phase 2 · Build

Responsive Website Development

Pages / Sections

  • Homepage: clear business introduction, parts enquiry CTA, customer types, fulfilment options, and service area messaging.
  • Parts focus: section for the types of parts the business wants to highlight, such as imported, hard-to-source, OEM/genuine, aftermarket, or specialist parts once confirmed.
  • About: short business background written to support the independent brand positioning.
  • Service areas: Illawarra, South Coast, Sydney, and Australia-wide shipping.
  • Contact / enquiry: prominent form and contact details.

Parts Enquiry Form

  • Customer name, business name, email, and phone.
  • Vehicle details: registration, VIN, make, model, year.
  • Part required, preferred part type if relevant, urgency, and message.
  • Pickup, delivery, or shipping preference.
  • Form submissions delivered to info@illawarrastarautoparts.com.

Technical Setup

  • Mobile-friendly responsive build.
  • Basic SEO metadata for auto parts, Illawarra, South Coast, Sydney, and Australia-wide shipping intent.
  • Domain connection once access or DNS support is available.
  • Basic launch QA: responsive layout, links, form delivery, and metadata check.

Optional Add-on

Logo Design

Optional

This add-on creates a logo identity for Illawarra Star Auto Parts before the website design is finalised.

The first round is used to choose the strongest visual direction, not to approve a final logo immediately. Once one direction is selected, typography, spacing, proportions, colours, and final file versions are refined from that direction.

Logo Process

  • Brief and references: confirm name, tone, preferred style, colours to use or avoid, and 3-5 reference brands if available.
  • Initial concept round: up to 3 simple logo directions, presented as visual directions rather than final logos.
  • Selected direction: the client chooses one direction to develop.
  • Refinement: up to 2 refinement rounds on the selected direction.
  • Final files: supplied in practical web-ready formats, including editable vector PDF and PNG, with colour and one-colour versions where appropriate.
Refinement rounds apply to the selected direction. If none of the initial directions are selected, an additional concept round can be added at an agreed extra cost.
