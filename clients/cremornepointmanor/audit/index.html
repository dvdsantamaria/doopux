The biggest missed opportunity in the entire profile

Every single Reel is silent. No voiceover, no spoken CTA, no narration. Instagram 2026 transcribes audio automatically and uses it to index content. Adding spoken audio to Reels is the single highest-leverage change available right now.

The most valuable asset that is not being used

Cremorne Point Manor has a unique location on the North Shore: the penthouse has direct views of the Harbour Bridge and the Opera House, while most rooms overlook Mosman Bay. That is a world class differentiator that currently represents only 10% of published content. A boutique hotel with this environment should feature that landscape in at least 30% to 40% of its posts. The opportunity is not to create something new. The opportunity is to show what already exists more effectively.
