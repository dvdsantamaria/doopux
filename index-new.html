Our methodology

Discovery & Business Analysis

We start with your real workflows and business logic. Stakeholder interviews, process mapping, pain-point ranking, and a clear-eyed assessment of what to build, fix, or automate, before any tool or platform is chosen.

Frequently asked questions

What kind of teams does Doop UX partner with?
We collaborate with founders, marketing leaders, and product squads that want enterprise-level execution without the layers of a traditional agency.
How fast can a new project launch with Doop UX?
Discovery to launch typically happens in four to six weeks thanks to lean sprints that validate direction quickly and keep momentum visible for stakeholders.
Can you integrate AI automation into our existing stack?
Yes, we map current workflows and deploy AI agents, automation, and data bridges across tools like Zapier, Make, n8n, and custom APIs while keeping governance intact.
What results should we expect from a typical engagement?
Clients see faster user journeys, measurable lift in qualified conversions, and a maintainable design system that scales without technical debt collecting in the background.
How do you keep communication clear once the project is underway?
We operate with transparent weekly readouts, async Loom recaps, and shared dashboards so decisions, blockers, and next steps remain obvious to every stakeholder.
