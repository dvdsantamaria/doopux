ECO vs Shopify

A different deal, not a cheaper clone.

Shopify is an excellent closed platform if you want speed and a huge app marketplace. ECO is for businesses that want to own their store and adapt it to how they actually work.

Closed platform

Shopify & co.

  • Monthly platform subscription to keep selling.
  • Paid apps for many specific behaviours.
  • You operate inside their environment and limits.
  • Code customisation limited by the platform.
Your instance

ECO by Doop

  • No monthly ecommerce platform subscription.
  • Your own installed instance: code, data, domain.
  • Adapted to your real business flow, not generic.
  • Start on low-cost infrastructure and scale.

Stripe charges its own per-transaction fees, and external costs like domain, email or SMS may apply. The point isn't "no costs", it's no platform rent.
